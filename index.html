// 01 — about the studio

Small team.
Loud output.

XENO Studio started as a one-person Roblox project and grew into a small distributed team that still ships like one. No quarterly roadmaps nobody reads — just builds, betas, and honest updates, posted as they happen.

What we make

Action, racing, and social experiences built primarily in Roblox Studio, with experiments in Unity and Godot when a concept needs more room.

How we work

Public playtests, open patch notes, and a community that shapes direction in real time — not after launch.

Why the feed matters

Every announcement, win, and delay gets posted here first — before social, before press. This page is the source of truth.
